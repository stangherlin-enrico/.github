## Reporting a vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in any of my repositories, **please do not open a public issue**.

Instead, report it privately by email: **enrico.stangherlin.1@studenti.unipd.it**

Include as much detail as possible:

- The affected repository and version / commit
- A description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce or a proof of concept
- Potential impact

I will acknowledge your report within 72 hours and work with you to address the issue before any public disclosure.

## Scope

This policy applies to all public repositories under the [`stangherlin-enrico`](https://github.com/stangherlin-enrico) account.
